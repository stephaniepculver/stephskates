You've been working hard. Coaches say your skating needs work but can't tell you what to fix. Stephanie can.

In 90 minutes on the ice, she'll break down your blade contact, weight transfer, and edge mechanics with the precision of a world-level figure skating judge. This isn't general skating advice — it's a diagnosis built specifically for your game.

The Edge Audit is a tangible, low-risk first step. You walk away with a written Performance Report — a clear, specific document outlining exactly what was found and what would unlock your player's next level.
