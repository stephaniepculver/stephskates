What This Is

Founding cohort access is different from the standard program.

The founding cohort of The Edge Protocol is a structured, bounded engagement for hockey players in Minneapolis-St. Paul, designed to:

  • Build documented outcomes and video case studies with a small, committed group of players
  • Give founding families direct coaching access at a level that will not be available at scale
  • Validate the program structure and establish the measurable benchmarks used in the full program launch

This is not a discounted version of the program. It is the same individualized coaching methodology — with more direct founder access, more documentation, and investment terms that will not be offered after the first cohort.

Why a founding cohort?

Before opening the program at full scale, we are working with a small group of committed players to document outcomes, build case studies, and prove the benchmarks. Founding families get more direct access. We get documented results.

When the founding cohort fills, the next group enters the standard program at the full rate. There is no guarantee a founding spot will be available if you wait.

What We Ask of Founding Families

A simple exchange for founder-level access.

The founding cohort model works because the documentation becomes proof — for the next family evaluating the program, and for the development of the full-scale launch. We ask two things in exchange for founding access:

Written Testimonial

A 2–3 sentence written testimonial at program completion, for use on the website and social content. No specific outcome is guaranteed — we ask for an honest account of the experience.

Session Footage Permission

Permission to use anonymized session footage for educational content. Player-optional — faces can be omitted. We will never identify your player without explicit consent.

Both asks are structured so that you retain full control. Nothing is shared publicly without your sign-off. The testimonial request comes at program completion, after you have seen the results.
