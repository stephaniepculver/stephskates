About Stephanie

Edges First. Always.

Stephanie Culver competed at the highest levels of figure skating, reaching 4th in the world — a result built on edge mastery that most coaches never teach and most skaters never learn.

She's watched elite talent plateau because no one addressed the foundational problem: edges. Not choreography. Not costume. Not conditioning. Edges.

"The gap between good and elite is almost always technical. And technical almost always means edges."

Through structured assessment and targeted intervention, Stephanie identifies exactly what is limiting each skater's ceiling — and fixes it with a precision that camps simply cannot deliver.
